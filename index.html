Robotics


By Omair


What Is This Technology?


Robotics is the blend of both computer science and engineering that focuses on designing, building, and programming robots. A robot is a machine that can do tasks automatically or with someone’s guidance to support or replace human labour.


What Problem Does This Technology Solve?


Robotics helps solve problems by making tasks less dangerous, faster, and less tedious for humans. Robots are able to work in places that can be very dangerous for a person such as handling harmful chemicals, making the job far more safer for humans. These machines can also work in factories to make tasks much more precise and quicker than the average human can do over long hours.


How Does It Roughly Work?


Robotics works by combining sensors, control systems, and mechanical parts so that a robot can perform its actions. The process of the robot starts off by gathering information from its environment, using sensors like a microphone or a camera. This information can then be processed by the robot and it will decide what to do next based on its code. The robot can then act with their decisions by using output devices like a motor to do what they intended.


Where Is It Used Today?


Robotics is now used in many industries where it improves various areas of everyday life. An industry that commonly uses robotics are manufacturing businesses. Places like factories require robots in packaging, repairing, or assembling a product. Robotics is also used in the medical field, where it can assist doctors through surgeries and x-ray scans. Additionally, space exploration regularly uses robots such as rovers and mechanical arms to assist astronauts in their ventures.


What Are Its Impacts?


Robotics has both positive and negative impacts in many aspects of human lives. Robots are much better than humans when it comes to certain tasks that involve accuracy and efficiency, increasing productivity in many businesses. Robotics also supports people in their daily lives like using a robot vacuum that can help people clean their surroundings and save them a lot of time. In contrast, many people may lose their jobs by the replacement of robots since they can do their tasks much better. Robots also require a lot of raw material and power to operate, which can increase pollution and electronic waste.
